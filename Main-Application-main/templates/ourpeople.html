About Techsnap

Established in 2021, Originated from the idea that there is no particular Information guide to beginners about the Technologies when they start to learn and there exists a class of beginners who are stuck Half way. So Here we are to provide you clean information about the technologies and Path to learn and master the technology with upskilled instructors, Innovative Roadmaps,Tutorials, Hands-on practice projects and lot many things.

Our Mission

Is - to create a clear roadmap for every course in computer-science, to help users learn programming more easily, to Advance teaching and learning through research, to Increase access to high-quality education for everyone and everywhere, to make students interview ready through 1-on-1 mentoring sessions with real-life interviewers, to offer high quality services at the most affordable prices for emerging startups.
